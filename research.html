Research

These are my research topics, the things I simulate.

My research is concerned with the dynamics and structure of stellar winds. I sumlate the magnetic behaviour of solar like stars as well as stars many times more massive than our sun. Simulating these winds allows us to understand how stars interact with one another, their planets and also the extended enviroment in which they live.

Winds from Massive Stars

Large stars with masses typically above 5 solar masses have winds in which the driving mechanism is the absorption in spectral lines. This means that the intensity of radiation produced by atomic transitions of heavy elements in the stellar atmosphere, imparts enough momentum to the wind material to form the dominant driving force. Such winds can reach terminal velocities above 3500 km/s.
